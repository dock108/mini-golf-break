Mini Golf Break

A fun, casual offline mini-golf experience in space

How to Play:

  • Click and drag backwards from the ball to set direction and power
  • Release to hit the ball
  • Use right-click drag to rotate the camera
  • Scroll to zoom in/out
  • Get the ball into the hole with as few strokes as possible
  • Press 'd' to toggle debug mode
